Cong Ding

I'm Cong Ding (IPA: [t͡sʰʊŋ tiəŋ], Chinese: 丁聪), an incoming software engineer at Anyscale. I graduated from University of Wisconsin-Madison with a Master's Degree in Computer Sciences. I got my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at Peking University, with a dual degree in Chinese Literature.

Previously, I collaborated with Prof. Shivaram Venkataraman on DBMS configuration tuning. I was also fortunate to work with Prof. Aaron Elmore and Prof. Sanjay Krishnan at UChicago, Prof. Tao Xie at Peking University and Prof. Stratos Idreos at Harvard.
